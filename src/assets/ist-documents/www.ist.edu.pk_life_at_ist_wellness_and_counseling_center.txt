Wellness and Counseling Center, IST is dedicated to help students, faculty and staff members maximize their sense of overall well-being and facilitate them towards making healthy contributions to their academic, personal, social and career development. The Centre provides free and confidential on- campus psychological counseling services, both in individual or group settings, where the students address their concerns with a highly trained mental health clinician in a safe and non-judgmental environment. The Centre further aims to highlight social stigma and spread awareness regarding various topics of mental health through workshops, advocacy and outreach programs.
