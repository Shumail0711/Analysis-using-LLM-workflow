IST Safety Department is responsible for developing and maintaining a safe and secure environment for the employees and students so that they can pursue their academic, professional and personal ambitions. Committed to serving with professional excellence, we strive to be approachable, reliable and truly service-oriented in the performance of our duties. The department maintains strong, professional relationships with all stake holders to ensure continuous collaboration on emergency planning efforts and familiarity with our campus in the event of an emergency. The following facilities are available at IST for Safety of individuals and premises during emergency. All buildings of IST have dedicated Emergency Exits and are also equipped with fire extinguishers, Water Hydrants. In addition, some areas e.g. data center are equipped with latest firefighting system (FM-200), smoke and heat detectors. IST also has an in-house, highly trained firefighting team that is equipped with latest Fire Tenders and other modern firefighting equipment.
