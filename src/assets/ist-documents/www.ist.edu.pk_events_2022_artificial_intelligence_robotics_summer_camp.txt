Institute of Space Technology (IST) is organizing an exciting 'Artificial Intelligence & Robotics Summer Camp' for kids. It is the 2nd Summer Camp of the series that will be even bigger & better than the first one held last year. During the camp, kids will be introduced to the amazing feats of AI & Robotics using interactive learning and fun filled activities. Kids will also get to design their robots via hands-on training using robotics kits. Kids will be divided into groups based on their age.
