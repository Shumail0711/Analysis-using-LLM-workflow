General Collection IST library has a collection of more than 11,900 books on all subjects relevant to the courses taught at the institute. Moreover, books on general knowledge, Islam, history, geography and fiction etc are also available. Reference Collection The reference section has a good collection of reference books including reference text, handbooks, encyclopedia and dictionaries etc. Periodicals IST library is currently subscribing to 33 journals / magazines to meet the requirements of researchers, faculty and students. Online Resources To enrich the library collection with latest online resources available through Internet, publications from professional societies are also accessible. Audio Visual Collection Audio-visual material is considered an essential medium of instruction. Library has a good collection of CDs, DVDs, Audio/Video Cassettes and related audio/video devices. Newspapers Library is currently subscribing to six newspapers to meet the requirements of researchers, faculty members and students.
