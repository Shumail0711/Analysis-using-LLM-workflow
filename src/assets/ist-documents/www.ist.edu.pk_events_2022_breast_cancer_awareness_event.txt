IST Character Building Society, in collaboration with Department of Student Affairs, conducted an awareness session on breast cancer on Wednesday, October 26, 2022. The aim of this event was to shed light on the causes, symptoms, self-examination process, and treatment options available for breast cancer, along with importance of healthy diet in preventing cancer. Dr Humera Naz Altaf, Assistant Professor, Shifa College of Medicine, General Surgeon Shifa international Hospital talked about causes, symptoms and available cure for breast cancer followed by a question-answer session. The guest speaker was presented a souvenir on behalf of IST.
