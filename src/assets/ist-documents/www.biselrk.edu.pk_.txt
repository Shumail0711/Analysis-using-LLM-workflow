It gives me immense pleasure to forward this special message to the students, teachers, parents and the people that, the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Larkana is launched its own Website on internet. In the era of globalization, no organization can fully develop without embracing the modern scientific methods. Our Website would be a big leap forward in the use of information technology by this Board, and it is visualized that through this Website the working of the Board will improve significantly.
