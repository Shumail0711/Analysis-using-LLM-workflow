Welcome to BISE Dera Ismail Khan BISE Dera Ismail Khan came into being in 2006. It is working in a building located on River Road , Dera Ismail Khan. The Examination conduct section consists of two separate sections. Matric Section to deal with matric exams and Intermediate Section to deal with intermediate exams. A printing section has also been created for printing of question papers. Computer Cell deals all data management activities. The Board is governed by a Calendar based on KPK, BISE Act 1990. The act along with regulations and rules provide a complete compendium of instructions for running the Board. Within the parameters laid down by this act, the Board makes rules and regulations for its running. In addition the Controlling Authority also has the powers to provide directions to the Board to regulate any activity. The jurisdiction of the Board consists of: 1: District Dera Ismail Khan and its FATA Regions. 2: District Tank and its FATA Regions. 3: South Waziristan Agency (S.W.A) Institutions including: a. Government schools, including Higher Secondary Schools and colleges, Tehsil-wise, both male and female. b. Private Schools, male and female, Tehsil-wise. c. Private Colleges, male and female, Tehsil-wise. All students of 9th and 11th class are required to be enroled/ registered with the Board.
