The Department of Materials Science and Engineering at the Institute of Space Technology (IST) is home to a distinguished faculty with expertise in various areas of materials science. Dr. Abdul Wadood, the Head of Department, obtained his Ph.D. in Shape Memory Materials from the Tokyo Institute of Technology (TITECH) Japan, with post-doctoral experience in High Temperature Shape Memory Materials from the High Temperature Materials Unit, NIMS, Tsukuba Japan. His areas of specialization include Shape Memory and Super-Elastic Materials, Biomaterials, High Temperature Materials, Corrosion, and Oxidation.

Dr. Anjum Tauqir, a Professor in the department, holds a Ph.D. in Metallurgy from the Institute of Materials Science, University of Connecticut, USA, specializing in Metallurgy. Another Professor, Dr. Syed Wilayat Husain, obtained his Ph.D. in Materials from the University of Connecticut, USA, with a focus on Phase Transformation & Alloy Development.

Associate Professor Dr. Abdul Faheem Khan earned his Ph.D. in Materials Science & Engineering from PIEAS, Islamabad, Pakistan, specializing in Nanostructured Multi-Layer Thin Film Solar Cells. Dr. Saad Nauman, also an Associate Professor, holds a Ph.D. in Carbon Composites from Universite des Sciences et Technologies de Lille 1 Lille, France, focusing on 3D Woven Carbon Composites & their Structural Health Monitoring. Dr. Saima Shabbir, another Associate Professor, has a Ph.D. in Chemistry from QAU, Islamabad, specializing in Polymer Chemistry.

Assistant Professors in the department include Dr. Muhammad Abdul Basit, who holds a Ph.D. in Materials Engineering from Hanyang University, South Korea, focusing on Advanced Materials for Energy Applications. Dr. Muhammad Atiq Ur Rehman obtained his Ph.D. in Materials Science & Engineering (EPD based Coatings) from the Institute of Biomaterials, University of Erlangen-Nuremberg, Germany, specializing in Biomaterials, Heat treatment, and Phase Transformations. Dr. Muhammad Yasir earned his Ph.D. in Materials Science & Engineering (Thermal Spray Coatings) from Huazhong University of Science & Technology, Wuhan China, with a specialization in Materials Science and Engineering.

The department's research associates and teaching/research associates contribute significantly to its academic environment and research endeavors. Their diverse backgrounds and areas of specialization, ranging from Material Characterization to Polymers & Polymeric Composites, enrich the department's research and educational programs, ensuring a well-rounded learning experience for students.
