This course focuses on periodic data collection and analysis for condition based maintenance programs. A sound foundation is established for in-depth understanding of spectrum and waveform relationships. The participants will develop good understanding of the fundamentals of vibrations and understand how to take good measurements in terms of repeatability and reliability. They will gain hands on practical experience regarding analyzing vibration spectra.This course is intended for personnel who are new to vibration monitoring and analysis or who have limited vibration analysis experience.
