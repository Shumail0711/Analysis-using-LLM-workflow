To get admission in ist Applicants can appear in any one of the below mentioned tests and can use the higher score to apply for admission in IST. Please note that an applicant must have minimum 33% obtained marks in entry test to apply for admission in IST: NAT which is conducted by NTS in every month. HAT which is conducted by ETC-HEC.
The Institute of Space Technology (also known as IST) is a public university located in Islamabad, Pakistan.
IST offers merit scholarships to three students (excluding sponsored students) in each discipline as per the existing policy.
To apply in IST the merit required is For FSc/ DAE Students:
Matric/ Equivalent is 20%
FSc Part-1 or DAE (03 years aggregate marks) is 40%
Entry Test is 40%
IST accept NAT/ HAT/ SAT-II score which is conducted by NTS/ HEC/ College board.
To pay IST fee Candidates fulfilling the admissions requirements and willing to join IST are required to deposit the fee in any online branch of HBL within due date mentioned on challan forms.
In 2024 Institute of Space Technology ranked 57th in Pakistan, 5836th in the global 2024 rating, and scored in the TOP 50% across 16 research topics.
IST is managing 03 x hostels for boys and 1 x hostel for girls. Students are expected to bring decent colour linen and their amenities with them at the time of joining. Boarding facility will only be given depending on availability of rooms.
The test which is required to get admission in IST is that it is mandatory for MS/ PhD applicants to pass the GAT/ GRE/ HAT in the relevant field of study with required marks/ score to apply for admission in IST. However, provisional admission without GAT/ GRE/ HAT may be granted only for first semester subject to availability of seats.
