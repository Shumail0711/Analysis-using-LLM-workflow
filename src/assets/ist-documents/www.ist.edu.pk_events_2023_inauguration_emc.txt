The esteemed Vice Chancellor of the Institute of Space Technology (IST) inaugurated the EMC Solution Lab, a pioneering addition to the Avionics Engineering Department. This momentous event witnessed the esteemed presence of faculty members from the Avionics department alongside Heads of Departments from various academic domains. The inauguration commenced with the recitation of the Holy Quran, followed by a warm welcome extended by Air Commodore Sardar Ahmed. He provided a comprehensive overview of the lab's objectives and significance. Technical insights were then shared by Air Commodore Irfan Majid, shedding light on the specialized EMC/EMI test facility. Addressing the audience, the Vice Chancellor of IST lauded the Avionics department's relentless dedication in establishing this cutting-edge EMC/EMI test facility, acknowledging its pivotal role in advancing the field. The event marked a significant milestone in the pursuit of excellence and innovation within the Avionics Engineering Department at IST.
