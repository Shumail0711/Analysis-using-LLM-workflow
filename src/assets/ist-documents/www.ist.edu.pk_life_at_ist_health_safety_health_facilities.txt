Health care is an important and necessary part of any organization. The true mark of an organization is how it looks after its employees and others. Institute of Space Technology provides medical cover to its employees and students. Medical cover consists of Professional man-power, MI Room, First Aid Facility, Ambulance for 24 hours and First Aid boxes at different locations. The employees of Institute and their families are entitled to free medical treatment from panel hospitals. IST is providing necessary Information, Instructions and training regarding Health, Safety and Environment to all at IST Campus.
