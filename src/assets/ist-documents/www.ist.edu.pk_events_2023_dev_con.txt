The Business Incubation Center at the Institute of Space Technology in collaboration with IST Entrepreneurial Society, ACM & GDSC proudly hosted DevCon 2.0, a dynamic competition that unfolded over the span of November 24th to November 25th, 2023. The event commenced with an inspiring opening address by Dr. Professor Ibrahim Qazi, Dean of IST, followed by esteemed guest speakers: Mr. Assad Saeed, Principal Technology Solution & Cloud Architect at Oracle Mr. Sajeel Shahzad, Oracle Academy Country Manager at Oracle Ms. Hira Zainab, Vice President of Strategic Affairs at Contour Software A captivating interactive mentoring session led by industry leaders, including Mr. Manzar Soofi, CEO & Founder of Sb-Mc Pvt Ltd, Mr. Zeeshan Qedwaee, Founder of Tech Comrad, and Mr. Ibtisam Babar, Regional Manager Special Initiatives at Zindigi, further enriched the event, leaving a lasting impact on our engaged audience. DevCon 2.0 provided a platform for university students nationwide to showcase their innovative ideas in three distinct competitions: Code-Runner, Space Cup, and Innovate-a-Thon, evaluated by an esteemed industrial jury Syyed Ahmed Masud CEO at Change Mechanics (Pvt) Ltd Dr. Waqas Ahmed Janjua, Assistant Professor at Air University, Syed Taimoor Hassan Director BIC Foundation University Dr Iftekhar Yezdani: Founder RaheQamar, Founder Elements Interactive Malik Shahzada Sohail Awan CEO Inflight Xpress Wehaaj Ahmed Sulehri Manager ORIC Air University We extend our heartfelt gratitude to our esteemed speakers, mentors, judges, and especially our sponsors - ID-ware, Change Mechanics, Zindigi, JS bank and bio Material labs - whose unwavering support contributed significantly to the resounding success of this event.
