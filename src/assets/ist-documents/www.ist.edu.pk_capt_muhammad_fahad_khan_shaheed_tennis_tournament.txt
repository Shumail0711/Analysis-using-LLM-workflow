Institute of Space Technology, Islamabad organized Capt. Muhammad Fahad Khan Shaheed Tennis Tournament at IST Tennis Court on September 2, 2023. Akeel Khan Won against Barkat Ullah (6-4) (7-5) in the Single Final. Inam Ul Haq and Col Zia Uddin Won against Asad Khan and Qaiser Afzal (6-2) (6-2) in 45 plus Doubles Final. Vice Chancellor IST Major General Rehan Abdul Baqi HI (M) was the chief guest of the tournament and also played an exhibition match.
