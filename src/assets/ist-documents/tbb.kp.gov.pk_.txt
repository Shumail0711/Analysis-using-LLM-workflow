The Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Textbook Board has been established under NWFP (Now KP) Ordinance No. 1 of 1971. Its mandate is to make arrangements for production and publication of textbooks from Classes I to XII, supplementary reading material relating to textbooks, production of reference and research material, and conduct of research in the field of curricula, textbooks and other reading material. After the adoption and introduction of the new Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Textbooks and Learning Materials (KPTLM) Policy 2017, the role of Textbook Board has become more important as a regulatory authority. The prime objective of the KP Textbook Board is timely availability of quality textbooks to all the students of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (Class I to XII) at minimum Cost. The KP Textbook Board has initiated process for automation of its activities for smooth and active response. It is hoped that computerization of the Board's system will bring a lot of improvement in its functioning.
