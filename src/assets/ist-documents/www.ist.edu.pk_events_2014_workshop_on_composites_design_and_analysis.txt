The training is targeted for designers, researchers, scientists and manager working in the field of mechanical, automotive, aerospace, civil, sports goods, armor protection and general engineering industries. The workshop will consist of 48 hours of class room teaching spread over 16 weeks every Monday and Wednesday, 1700-1830 hours starting 15 Sep, 2014 as part of graduate course. It will be held in the serene environment of Institute of Space Technology, Islamabad, Pakistan. The participants must have sound knowledge of Mechanics, Mathematics and will get certificate at the end of workshop.
