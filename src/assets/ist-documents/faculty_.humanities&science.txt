The Department of Humanities & Sciences at the Institute of Space Technology (IST) boasts a dedicated faculty with expertise in various fields. Ausima Sultan Malik, the Head of Department, holds a Ph.D. from Foundation University, Islamabad, specializing in Applied Linguistics and Literature. Syeda Aysha Bokhari, an Assistant Professor, also holds a Ph.D. from Foundation University, Islamabad, with a focus on Applied Linguistics and Literature.

Syed Ubaidullah Jamil, another Assistant Professor in the department of Humanities & Sciences, has an MS in Islamic Studies from IIUI, Islamabad, specializing in Aqeedah & Philosophy. Uzma Nasir, also an Assistant Professor, holds an M.Phil. in Linguistics & Literature from Air University, Islamabad, specializing in Linguistics & Literature.
