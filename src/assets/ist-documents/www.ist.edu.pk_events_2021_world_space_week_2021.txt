Institute of Space Technology (IST), Pakistan celebrated World Space Week (WSW) from October 4-10, 2021. An online ceremony was held to mark the inauguration of “Women in Space” themed World Space Week 2021, at Institute of Space Technology.Vice Chancellor - Institute of Space Technology - Maj. Gen. (Retd.) Rehan Abdul Baqi - HI (M) officiated the celebrations at IST. Speaking to the participating students and their faculty members, he especially commended the efforts of faculty for cultivating an interest in the young inquisitive minds for space technology and its applications. He highlighted the firm role of women in the advancement of space science and technology in Pakistan.More than 4000 participants from 100+ schools, colleges and universities of Pakistan participated in 191 space themed competitions, webinars and trainings in a weeklong activity being organized by Institute of Space Technology. The celebrations are in line with the United Nations deceleration of World Space Week from October 4-10, to educate people about the bounties of space and to encourage greater use of space technology for sustainable socio-economic development. This year the world is celebrating the accomplishments and contributions of women to the space sector. SUPARCO being the National Space Agency of Pakistan is the National Coordinator for World Space Week celebrations.
