This department is among the few offering graduate and undergraduate degrees in Aerospace Engineering in Pakistan. Education in Aerospace Engineering at this department focuses on imparting the knowledge to a level that our graduates are successful in their professional careers, including industry, research organizations, government service and academia. Our program prepares students for amicable application of engineering principles, processes and practices to Aerospace systems and their maintenance. We prepare our students to contribute effectively when they have to work as member of an integrated team through effective communication with other team members, supervisors, and clients. The programs inculcate among students a strong sense of civic, professional and ethical responsibility. In a short span since inception, we have made our place among the top engineering institutions of the country. We are proud that today our graduates are successfully contributing in the development of new products, conduct of original research and implementation of solutions to relevant field problems in various disciplines of Engineering in country and abroad. Our programs strive to develop innovation capacity along with passion for lifelong learning. The department has faculty members with specialization, research and diversified experience in design, maintenance and manufacturing. The standards and outcome of technical projects undertaken by the students itself speaks high of their acquired knowledge.We welcome your interest to discover the new horizons in Aeronautics & Astronautics and pursue your career in this dynamic learning environment.
