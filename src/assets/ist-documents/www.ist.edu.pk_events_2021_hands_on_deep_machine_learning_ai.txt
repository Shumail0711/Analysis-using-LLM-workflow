The workshop on AI & Deep Machine Learning was successfully conducted at iVision Lab of Institute of Space Technology. A total of 24 participants attended the workshop.Institute of Space Technology (IST) is conducting a hands on CPD workshop on Deep Machine Learning & AI which will be delivered over 6 sessions in 2 days on 15-16 February 2021 from 9:00am to 4:00 pm PKT. In this workshop, you’ll learn how deep learning works through hands-on exercises. You’ll train deep learning models from scratch, learning tools and tricks to achieve highly accurate results.
