The Cyber & Information Security Lab at the Institute of Space Technology is proudly hosting a two-day workshop on “Reverse Engineering Cyber Weapons:” from 25th to 26th March 2024. The participants will gain hands-on experience with integrated labs on dissecting malicious software and shell codes. They will learn how to analyze malware, including computer viruses, trojans, and rootkits, using disassemblers, debuggers, static and dynamic analysis.
