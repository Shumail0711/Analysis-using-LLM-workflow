The Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Institute of Space Technology (IST) comprises a distinguished faculty with diverse expertise. Asif Israr, Professor and Head of Department, holds a Ph.D. from the University of Glasgow, UK, specializing in dynamics. He is an HEC Approved Ph.D. Supervisor. Muhammad Zubair, a Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, earned his Ph.D. from Brunel University in Uxbridge, Middlesex, West London, England (UK), specializing in dynamics. He is also an HEC Approved Ph.D. Supervisor.

Owais ur Rehman Shah, an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, holds a Ph.D. from the University of BREST, France, specializing in failure in composites. He is an HEC Approved Ph.D. Supervisor. Muhammad Umer, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, earned his Ph.D. from Politecnico Di Torino, Italy, specializing in non-linear vibration dynamics and machine design.

Naseem Ahmad, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, holds a Ph.D. from Harbin Engineering University, China, specializing in aerodynamics of turbomachinery, turbine blade cooling, flutter, and computational fluid dynamics. He is an HEC Approved Ph.D. Supervisor. Talha Irfan Khan, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, earned his Ph.D. from Koc University, Turkey, specializing in computational fluid dynamics and biomedical applications.

Usama Waleed Qazi, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, holds a Ph.D. from the University of Engineering and Technology, Taxila, specializing in engineering management. He is an HEC Approved Ph.D. Supervisor. Usman Ghafoor, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, earned his Ph.D. from Pusan National University, Republic of Korea, specializing in signal processing and controls, robotics, and brain imaging.

Yumna Qureshi, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, holds a Ph.D. from ENSTA Bretagne, France, specializing in mechanics of solids, materials, structures, and surfaces. She is an HEC Approved Ph.D. Supervisor. Muhammad Azfar Jamal and Ruqia Ikram, both Lecturers in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, hold MS degrees from IST, Islamabad, specializing in mechanical design and analysis.

In addition to the academic staff, the department also boasts a dedicated team of researchers and lab engineers. Adnan Khan, Arsalan Ahsan Shaikh, Hafiz Mumtaz Alam, Muhammad Jazib, and Sultan Fateh Ali Khan serve as Senior Research Associates, each bringing expertise in various aspects of mechanical engineering. The team of Lab Engineers, including Abubakar Talal, Ahsan Ayaz, Ahsan Ghafoor, Hanzla Shahid, and Saad Akram, contribute to the practical aspects of mechanical engineering education and research at IST. Muhammad Junaid, Muhammad Shahid Latif, Muhammad Umar, Syed Afaq Ali Shah, and Syed Muhammad Ali Shah contribute as Research Associates, bringing valuable insights and contributions to the department's research endeavors.
