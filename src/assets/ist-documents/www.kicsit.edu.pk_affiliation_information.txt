KICSIT has been declared as Campus of Institute of Space & Technology, Islamabad with effect from Fall-2019. From Fall 2019, admissions have been made through IST, Islamabad. KICSIT is currently offering two programs as following. BSCE (Bachelors of Sciences in Computer Engineering), 04 years degree program. BSCE is accredited with PEC Pakistan Engineering Council. BSCS (Bachelors of Sciences in Computer Science), 04 years degree program previously and approved by National Computing Education Accreditations Council (NCEAC Dr. A. Q. Khan Institute of Computer Sciences and Information Technology (KICSIT), Kahuta was approved and inaugurated in November 2000 by none other than Dr. A. Q. Khan himself, the founder and then chairman of KRL. Initially, KICSIT offered Bachelor of Computer Science (BSCS) program in affiliation with Gomal University, D I Khan. Later in 2001, 4-year Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology (BEIT) program was started in affiliation with University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Taxila. For the purpose of accreditation since Spring 2013 BEIT program was converted into BSIT (Bachelor of Science in Information Technology). As per the direction of HEC to UET Taxila, BSIT program being not offered in UET Taxila itself, hence it was converted into BSCS (Bachelors of Sciences in Computer Science). The Previous batches of BSCS and BSCE will remain on the rolls of UET Taxila as per the agreement.
