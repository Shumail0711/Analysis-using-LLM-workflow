The state of AJ&K also established directorate of Curriculum and Research Development (DCRD) which is responsible for curriculum and research development in the State in AJ&K. This directorate is headed by DPI (BPS-20) and assisted by one Deputy Director in supervising 6 districts education Institutes for Teacher Education (3 for males and 3 for females). It also reviews the work of Subject Specialists, and other supporting staff through Deputy Director Training while the Deputy Director (Admin.) supervises the work of Audit, Librarian, Budget and Administrative Officer and Office Superintendent.
