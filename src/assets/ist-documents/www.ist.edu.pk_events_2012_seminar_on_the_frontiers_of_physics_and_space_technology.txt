Aug. 31- Sep. 1, 2012 02 Days Rs. 1000 It is well known that Pakistan science is not doing too well and does not seem to recover if left alone the way it is. In the past Government of Pakistan has made efforts at various stages. Recently the Higher Education Commission (HEC) has made a concentrated effort to promote science at the tertiary level, out of which some positive results have begun to emerge. Regretfully, however, there has been no governmental or non-governmental effort, made for Pakistan in leading the world, at least, in some field, if not all around. We ought to get started to do that right now by establishing first and foremost an infrastructure for Pakistan Science, which may produce scholars in the front echelon of their respective fields to lead the world.
