Going to IST is about making the most of your life as a student. As you get involved, you'll not only have a lot of fun, you'll also develop your leadership abilities, raise your consciousness, and acquire skills you'll use after graduation. Together students, faculty and staff of IST create a welcoming and enterprising community to achieve all this. List of student societies is always evolving with the changing interests of students. Are you interested in academics, athletics, socializing, social work, performing arts, gaming or movies? There's a good chance that a student society exists for what you want to do.
