BISE Kohat came into being on 19 January 2002 with the appointment of Brig (Retd) Talat Imtiaz Naqvi as the Founder Chairman. The Board started functioning in temporary offices in KDA Kohat. Simultaneously, 50 kanal land was procured on Indus Highway, adjacent to Kohat University of Science & Technology. The master plan and detailed drawings of the new Board Complex were approved by Lt Gen (Retd) Syed Iftikhar Hussain Shah, Governor Khyber Pakhtunkhwa/Controlling Authority in September 2002 and construction of the new complex commenced on 1 November 2002. The Board of Intermediate & Secondary Education, Kohat, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa emerged on the vista of educational spectra to nurture the challenging demands of 21st century which is yearning for the packing of mechanical and rote learning and to replace it with the conceptual clarity and critical thinking of the students, the future builders of the nation.
