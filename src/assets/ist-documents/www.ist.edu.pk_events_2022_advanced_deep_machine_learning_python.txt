Institute of Space Technology (IST) is conducting a hands on CPD workshop on Advanced Deep Machine Learning - Hands on using Python which will be delivered over 11 sessions in 3 days on 10-12 January 2022 from 9:30 am to 3:30 pm PKT. In this workshop, you’ll learn how advance deep learning works through hands-on exercises. You’ll train advance deep learning models from scratch, learning tools and tricks to achieve highly accurate results. You’ll also learn to leverage freely available, state-of-the-art pre-trained models to save time and get your deep learning application up and running quickly.
