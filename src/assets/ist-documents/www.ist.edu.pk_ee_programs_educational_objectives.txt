The Department of Electrical Engineering has designed following Educational Objectives for Electrical Engineering program through brainstorming with faculty and keeping in view of Departments' mission, Institute's Vision and stakeholders' requirements. The following PEOs have been defined in consultation with the Departmental Faculty Members and Industry Advisory Board (IAB). The graduates of Electrical Engineering program will have following capabilities/ skills / attributes four years after their graduation; PEO 1: Ability to investigate complex engineering problems using modern techniques and propose effective solutions. PEO 2: Manage projects and become effective members of engineering teams through inter-personal skills. PEO3: Demonstrate high moral and ethical values, life-long learning attitude and societal responsibilities.
