GIS day is a worldwide event that celebrates the technology of Geographic Information Systems (GIS). Geospatial Society at Institute of Space Technology, Islamabad brings second Geospatial Hackathon in affiliation with National Centre of GIS and Space Applications (NCGSA). This 3-day event aims to connect and empower students, Geospatial/GIS Analyst, Developers And Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) from all over Pakistan to design solutions for the world's toughest challenges being faced today. Participants will propose and develop geospatial solutions/prototypes to the problems and challenges faced by humanity these days in the domains of Sustainable Development Goals (5,6 & 13). SDG 5: Gender Equality "Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls." SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation "Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all." SDG 13: Climate Action "Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts." The Geohackathon will result in innovative and actionable GIS solutions to the world's most pressing sustainability concerns. Geohackathon 2021 is a 3 day event. Be ready to bring the change with geospatial solutions to world problems. Registration Fee: PKR 1000/- The last date to apply is 31st, January 2022 . National Center of GIS and Space Applications (NCGSA); a project of Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan led by IST, encompasses endeavors that strive to achieve and sustain human, technological, research and entrepreneurial capacity in the areas of space science, technology and its applications.
