Manufacturing being one of the crucial aspect of Mechanical Engineering has a key role in uplifting the nation's production capacity, economy, and national exports. Therefore, this institute has a very high responsibility to train its graduates in the area of design and manufacturing to meet the requirements of the local industry in line with the modern techniques. This program focuses on the area of Computer-Aided Manufacturing, knowledge about modern techniques and advanced processes widely in the industry, rapid manufacturing, tooling, fabrication methods, advanced manufacturing systems using IoT, and smart factory concepts.
