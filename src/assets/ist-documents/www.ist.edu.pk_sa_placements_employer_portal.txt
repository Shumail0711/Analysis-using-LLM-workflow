The Department of Student Affairs is engaged in empowering students by facilitating them for their placement in industry. For their professional development, this office organizes various placement orientated sessions for the students where they can learn about their career choices from professionals in different fields of interest. Technically sound and managerially efficient graduates of IST are ranked among top professionals by numerous employers. We make sure that our students get the best placement opportunities as soon as they graduate from IST. Every year at Open House, IST invites numerous employers from Public and Private organizations and provides a platform to fresh graduates for their placements.
