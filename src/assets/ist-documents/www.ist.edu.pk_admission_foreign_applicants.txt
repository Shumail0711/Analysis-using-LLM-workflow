Scholarships are available for Foreign Students to study in Bachelor/ Master/ Doctoral Degree Programs offered by IST. Duration of Scholarship: For BS (Bachelor’s) Students: Upto 04 years For MS (Master’s) Students: Upto 2.5 years For PhD (Doctoral) Students: Upto 04 years The scholarship program covers funds for the following expenses: Mandatory academic & non-academic charges like admission fee, endowment fund, library development fund, service charges, safe/ smart campus charges, hostel charges etc. are not covered. Candidates willing to apply for admission in BS, MS/ PhD programs offered by IST must: Incharge Admissions Focal Person (International Student Office) Email: admissions@ist.edu.pk Contact: +92-51-9075100
