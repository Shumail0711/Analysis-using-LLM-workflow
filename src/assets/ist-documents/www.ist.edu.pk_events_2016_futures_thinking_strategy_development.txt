To meet the challenges emerging due to globalization and advanced pace of technological development, it is required to equip our national policy makers, leaders, directors and senior managers working in public and private sectors, HEIs, industry and business with the latest tools and techniques of future thinking and policy making. The Institute of Space Technology in Collaboration with the Higher Education Commission is going to organize a workshop on "Futures Thinking and Policy Development". Prof. Sohail Inayatullah, a Pakistani-born Australian academic, futures studies researcher and Professor at the Graduate Institute of Futures Studies at Tamkang University , Taiwan and UNESCO Chair on Future Studies will be the resource person for the workshop. 19-21 Dec, 2016 (09:00 am - 05.00 pm daily) Institute of Space Technology, 1 Islamabad Highway Islamabad (Adjacent to DHA Phase II Islamabad) The authors are responsible for the choice and presentation of views contained in this workshop and opinions expressed therein, which are not necessarily those of UNESCO and do not commit the organization as well.
