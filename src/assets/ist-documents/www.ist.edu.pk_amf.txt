The Advanced Manufacturing Facility (AMF) at IST is established to provide top quality Precision Manufacturing services for R & D and innovative commercial solutions. The all-inclusive manufacturing setup at AMF boasts top of the line machining equipment, a team of highly trained technical experts, and a holistic design-centric philosophy perfected with experience. AMF complements CAE solutions with Computer Aided Manufacturing (CAM) programs through a well-rounded focus on multi-axis toolpath configuration, collision mitigation, and parameter optimization. Products manufactured at AMF are accurate to 0.001 mm and are thoroughly inspected via CMM before delivery to customers. The machining capabilities of AMF go well beyond what is offered in the market that includes fabrication and processing of hard-to-cut materials such as high-carbon steels, titanium, and nickel alloys. AMF has also installed an automation-based technology by incorporating a robot-actuated Computer Integrated Manufacturing setup that achieves remarkable productivity and accuracy by minimizing human intervention.
