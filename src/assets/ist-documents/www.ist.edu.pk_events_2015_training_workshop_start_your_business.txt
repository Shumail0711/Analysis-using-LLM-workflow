The Office of Research, Innovation and Commercialization (ORIC) in collaboration with International Labour Organization (ILO) and Higher Education Commission (HEC) is conducting a 5-day training workshop on Start Your Business (SYB) at IST on August 24-28, 2015. SYB is a training program for starting entrepreneurs, who have a feasible business idea for their own small enterprise. The objective of SYB training for the starting entrepreneur is to actually get started after developing a detailed bankable business plan. The plan provides all information required for securing access to the market, finance, human resources and all other business inputs. Interested final year students may get registered with the ORIC to attend.
